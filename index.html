Light The Bridge - Find Your Bulb

This site was created for Edmonton's Light the Bridge campaign, during which Edmontonians could adopt a light by donating to the city's goal of installing lights on the High Level Bridge. Find Your Bulb used an accurate scale SVG representation of the High Level Bridge with clickable lights mapped to it showing location of the donor's light. Donors could search by name or light dedication to find their own lights, or they could explore the bridge by panning and zooming across it to browse thousands of other donor lights and stories. The site was fully responsive and worked on touch devices.

I collaborated with ATB Financial's web team to design the user experience and the data architecture, and was responsible for coding the light mapping and interface interaction with javascript, HTML and CSS, while ensuring that the experience worked across devices.
